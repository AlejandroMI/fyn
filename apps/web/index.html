The Problem

Looking for a place is a nightmare. It doesn't have to be.

You know the drill. You install five different apps. You set up the exact same filters on every single one. You refresh constantly. You compromise. Dropdown menus cannot capture nuances like “I am open to renovations” or “a place with great views.”

Fyn fixes this by bringing the market into one searchable layer and letting you search the way you actually think.

Who Fyn Serves

Built for real decision moments.

Fyn is designed for the people who need it most: buyers under time pressure, dreamers looking for a lifestyle, and developers building the next generation of agents.

City Movers

Buyers and renters comparing neighborhoods fast.

They want clarity in minutes, not dozens of tabs and portal-specific filter logic.

  • Single intent prompt: Search in Spanish or English.

Lifestyle Seekers

Second-home and nature buyers searching across towns.

They care about atmosphere first and refine location after seeing real supply.

  • Multi-location search: Scan multiple regions in a single request.

  • Explainable notes: See exactly why each property matched.
AI Builders

Teams exposing property search through connectors and agents.

They need stable interfaces, not custom parsing per portal.

  • MCP tool shape: Optimized specifically for LLM planning.
  • Normalized response cards: Clean data across portal sources.
  • Clear diagnostics: Built-in fallback and coverage reporting.

How It Works

Simple flow. Talk to your AI, Fyn does the rest.

You do not need to learn a new interface. Plug Fyn into your AI and start chatting.

1

The model plans the search

You type what you want. Your AI chooses locations, strictness, and search breadth from your intent.

2

Fyn executes the connectors

Fyn runs portal searches, normalizes messy listing data into clean cards, and attaches diagnostics.

3

You decide faster

Compare clear cards in chat, see why each match appeared, and open the source listing immediately.
