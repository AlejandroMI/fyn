# Fyn privacy summary

Fyn processes the search query and filters submitted by a user to perform property discovery. Infrastructure providers may process technical data such as IP addresses, timestamps, and error logs to operate and protect the service. Do not include sensitive personal information in searches.

Fyn does not build user profiles or sell personal data. For the complete policy, visit [fynfyn.top/privacy](https://fynfyn.top/privacy) or contact `support@fynfyn.top`.
