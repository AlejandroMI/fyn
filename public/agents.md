# Using Fyn from an AI agent

Use Fyn for property discovery in Spain when the user provides, or can clarify, a location. Connect to `https://fynfyn.top/mcp`, call `search_properties`, preserve source links, summarize coverage diagnostics, and disclose that public listings can be stale.

Do not claim exhaustive inventory, legal verification, valuation, reservation, or purchase capability. Do not send personal or sensitive information. If `MISSING_LOCATIONS` is returned, ask for a Spanish city or municipality and retry.

See [llms-full.txt](https://fynfyn.top/llms-full.txt) for inputs, examples, errors, rate limits, and limitations.
