Who Fyn serves

Built for real decision moments.

The page now talks to the actual people we need first: buyers and renters under time pressure, lifestyle seekers, and builders integrating Fyn into agents.

City movers

Buyers and renters comparing neighborhoods fast.

They want clarity in minutes, not dozens of tabs and portal-specific filter logic.

  • Single intent prompt in ES or EN.
  • Constraint-first results for budget and rooms.
  • Direct jump to the original listing.
Lifestyle seekers

Second-home and nature buyers searching across towns.

They care about atmosphere first and can refine location after seeing real supply.

  • Multi-location search in one request.
  • Natural-language intents like “light”, “views”, “quiet”.
  • Explainable match notes per listing.
AI builders

Teams exposing property search through connectors and agents.

They need stable interfaces, not custom parsing per portal.

  • MCP tool shape optimized for LLM planning.
  • Normalized response cards across sources.
  • Clear diagnostics for fallback and coverage.

How it works

Simple flow, connector by connector.

1

Model plans the search

The LLM chooses locations, constraint strictness, and search breadth based on user intent.

2

Fyn executes connectors

MCP tools run portal searches, normalize cards, and attach diagnostics for transparency.

3

User decides faster

People compare cards, read why each match appears, and open the source listing immediately.
